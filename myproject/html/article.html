Message From Principal

30 Jun 04:12 PM Home > Blog > News > Admin Welcome ! Dear Parents and Students,

Greetings from Aryan School of Engineering and Management!

It is with immense pleasure and pride that I extend a warm welcome to each one of you. As the Principal of this esteemed institution, I am excited to share with you the vision and values that drive us to excel in the realm of education.

Aryan School of Engineering and Management, located in the heart of Kathmandu at Mid-Baneshwor, stands as a beacon of quality education and holistic development. Our affiliation with Purbanchal University speaks volumes about our commitment to academic excellence and adherence to the highest standards of education.

Dear students, your academic journey at Aryan School is an opportunity for personal growth, skill development, and the pursuit of knowledge. We offer four years of comprehensive undergraduate programs in BE Civil, BIT, BCA, and BBA, each carefully designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in your chosen field. Our experienced faculty members are dedicated mentors who will support and guide you throughout your academic pursuits. As an institution, we emphasize not only academic rigor but also co-curricular and extracurricular activities that nurture creativity, leadership, and teamwork. Our aim is to produce graduates who are not just academically proficient but also well-rounded individuals, ready to face the challenges of the professional world. We invite you to explore our website and visit our campus to experience the vibrant atmosphere of Aryan School of Engineering and Management. Thank you for choosing Aryan School, where we shape futures and inspire minds!
