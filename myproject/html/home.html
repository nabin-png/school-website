Our Programs

The Foundation established Ullens School at Khumaltar-15, Lalitpur in 2006. The school has been registered with the Ministry of Education and has obtained all the required permissions. In 2009 Ullens School also received authorization from MoE, Nepal and IBO (International Baccalaureate Organization) to run IBDP (International Baccalaureate Diploma Program) at Ullens School and IBDP came to full operation from August of the same year.The school has been registered with the Ministry of Education and has obtained all the required permissions.
